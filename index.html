About Me

I’m, Sahithi a high school senior at James B. Conant High School. I have a passion for research, computer science and healthcare. Since freshman year, I've participated in state or national competitions for research and won multiple awards. My papers are published in arXiv and pending publication in IEEE Xplore. Research

Brain Tumor Detection and Segmentation using Computer Vision, 2019-2020

presented at 2020 IEEE MIT URTC and will be published in IEEE Xplore Journal as part of proeceedings

The application is designed to take in 2D MRI images and will then detect and segment the tumor using a convolutional neural network. Specifically, I implemented a Mask R CNN segmentation model through transfer learning. I also used Google Cloud Services for 6 vCPUs and 60 GB memory Linux server running Ubuntu to train the neural network.

arXiv Slides Poster

Diagnosis of Heart Disease Using Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks, 2018-2019

presented at 2019 IEEE MIT URTC and Northwestern Undergraduate Research and Arts Exposition. It’s pending publication in IEEE Xplore as part of proceedings.

I developed an application for assistive heart disease diagnosis. I utilized Google TensorFlow and scikit-learn to develop machine learning models and neural networks. Then, I applied various optimization and hyper parametrization like the Grid Search Algorithm to increase the accuracy prediction rate of heart disease.

arXiv Northwestern Slides Poster

Correlation Between GPS Error Signals and Geomagnetic Activity in the Ionosphere, 2017-2018

presented at 2018 Northwestern Undergraduate Research and Arts Exposition

Investigated GPS errors and geomagnetic activity in the ionosphere to discover affected systems, helping industries relying heavily on geolocation devices
