Research

Brain Tumor Detection and Segmentation using Computer Vision, 2019-2020

Application is designed to take in 2D MRI images and will then detect and segment the tumor using Computer Vision. Specifically, a Mask R CNN segmentation model was implemented through transfer learning. I used Google Cloud VM Services to train and validate the model as well.

Paper Slides Poster

Diagnosis of Heart Disease Using Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks, 2018-2019

I used Google TensorFlow and scikit-learn to develop machine learning models and neural networks. Various optimization and hyper parametrization techniques were applied to increase the accuracy prediction rate of heart disease. At the end, an application was created for the assisted heart disease diagnosis.

arXiv Northwestern Slides Poster

Correlation Between GPS Error Signals and Geomagnetic Activity in the Ionosphere, 2017-2018

I investigated the correlation between GPS errors and geomagnetic activity in the ionosphere. Specifics of this correlation and the affected values/systems were discovered as well.
