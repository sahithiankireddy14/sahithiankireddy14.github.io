Research

Brain Tumor Detection and Segmentation using Computer Vision, 2019-2020

presented at 2020 IEEE MIT URTC and will be published in IEEE Xplore Journal as part of proeceedings

The application is designed to take in 2D MRI images and will then detect and segment the tumor using a convolutional neural network. Specifically, I implemented a Mask R CNN segmentation model through transfer learning. I also used Google Cloud VM Services to train and validate the model.

Paper Slides Poster

Diagnosis of Heart Disease Using Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks, 2018-2019

presented at 2019 IEEE MIT URTC and Northwestern Undergraduate Research and Arts Exposition

I used Google TensorFlow and scikit-learn to develop machine learning models and neural networks. I applied various optimization and hyper parametrization techniques to increase the accuracy prediction rate of heart disease. At the end, I created an application for assisted heart disease diagnosis, where doctors get input a patient's lab data and immediately get a result.

arXiv Northwestern Slides Poster

Correlation Between GPS Error Signals and Geomagnetic Activity in the Ionosphere, 2017-2018

presented at 2018 Northwestern Undergraduate Research and Arts Exposition

I investigated the correlation between GPS errors and geomagnetic activity in the ionosphere. Specifics of this correlation and the affected values/systems were discovered as well.
